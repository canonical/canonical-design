Openness benefits everyone

Open processes and products create higher quality results and unparalleled freedom to use, modify, and distribute.

This is why we are exposing our practice. We open source our design systems, publish our design thoughts and invite open discussion. We default to open for Canonical products and support other efforts in the open design space.

We encourage you to use our resources for your own open source projects, participate in designing our products, or reach out to us if you're ready to collaborate.

What's on this site

We welcome designers and open source enthusiasts to use our design assets and learn from our methods.

You can download brand resources, use our design system or learn about our design strategy and decisions. You can also meet our team, browse open positions or find an open source project to get started with.

About our team

We're a team of cross-collaborative specialists. We work across disciplines and value learning from each other and our community.

We take pride in our diverse backgrounds, perspectives and skills, and our culture of openly building on each others' strengths.

We work with some of the smartest engineers in (and across) the world to deliver highly innovative technical solutions that run critical infrastructure everywhere. Our job is to make sure everyone can access this exciting technology.

We are entirely remote but travel at least twice a year to meet somewhere in the world and work together.

Join our team to create some of the most meaningful design work of your career.
