Be part of the open design community

The Open Design Working Group (ODWG) at Canonical is dedicated to empowering external designers to contribute to open-source projects. We focus on bridging the gap between designers and project maintainers, making it easier for designers to get involved and for maintainers to receive valuable design input.

Join us in shaping the future of design within the open-source community — and don't forget to share the sauce!
